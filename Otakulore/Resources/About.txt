This app was during the free time by Dennise Catolos. The purpose of creating this app is so that otaku people, those loves to watch anime &amp; read manga, enjoys one place to enjoy all their daily entertainment. The name was conceived through combining the two words, "Otaku" & "Lore". The word, otaku, that describes people with comsuming interest, particularly in anime and manga. And the word, lore, meaning the body of knowledge on a particular subject.
